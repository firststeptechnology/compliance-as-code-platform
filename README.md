# Compliance-as-Code

> Machine-readable cybersecurity and compliance rule libraries for AI, DevSecOps, Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC), and compliance automation.

Developed and maintained by **First Step Technology LLC**.

---

## Overview

Compliance-as-Code transforms cybersecurity and regulatory requirements into structured, machine-readable formats that can be consumed by:

- AI coding assistants
- DevSecOps pipelines
- Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) platforms
- Internal audit workflows
- Security engineering teams
- Compliance automation solutions

Instead of manually interpreting regulatory text, organizations can integrate structured compliance guidance directly into their engineering and governance processes.

## Why Compliance-as-Code?

Traditional compliance documentation is designed for humans.

Modern engineering teams need compliance requirements that are:

- Structured
- Searchable
- Version-controlled
- Automatable
- AI-compatible

This repository demonstrates how cybersecurity frameworks can be represented as reusable data instead of static documents.
