# Code of Conduct

First Step Technology LLC is committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and inclusive community.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Be professional
- Focus on technical discussion
- Welcome differing viewpoints

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Spam
- Malicious activity

Maintainers reserve the right to remove content that violates these expectations.

Questions may be directed to:

https://firststeptechnology.com
