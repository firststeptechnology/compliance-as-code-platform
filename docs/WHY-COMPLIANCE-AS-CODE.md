# Why Compliance-as-Code?

Compliance requirements continue to grow in complexity across cybersecurity, privacy, artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and enterprise governance.

Traditional compliance processes often rely on spreadsheets, static documents, and manual reviews that can be difficult to maintain, automate, or scale.

Compliance-as-Code applies modern software engineering principles to compliance by representing regulatory requirements as structured, machine-readable data.

---

# Traditional Compliance

Traditional compliance activities often involve:

- Manual control reviews
- Spreadsheet tracking
- Static documentation
- Manual evidence collection
- Repetitive assessments
- Time-consuming reporting

While effective, these approaches can become difficult to scale across large organizations.

---

# Compliance-as-Code

Compliance-as-Code transforms compliance information into structured data that software can understand.

This enables organizations to:

- Automate validation
- Improve consistency
- Reduce manual effort
- Accelerate assessments
- Support continuous compliance
- Build AI-assisted workflows
- Standardize governance processes
