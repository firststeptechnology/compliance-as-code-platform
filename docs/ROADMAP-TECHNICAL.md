# Technical Roadmap

The First Step Technology LLC Compliance-as-Code Platform is being developed in phases to provide a scalable, machine-readable compliance ecosystem for developers, enterprises, AI systems, and Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) professionals.

---

# Vision

Build the industry's leading Compliance-as-Code platform by delivering standardized, machine-readable regulatory content that integrates seamlessly into modern software development, cloud, AI, and enterprise governance environments.
